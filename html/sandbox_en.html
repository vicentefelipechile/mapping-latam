Rules of the Sandbox server

Do not Spawnkill

Do not kill anyone inside or outside the spawn.

Do not make propblock

Do not block the passage of players with props.

Do not make Prop Kill/Push

Push or bother with props or even kill with a gravitational force against the affected player (in other words, you killed him with a prop).

Do not cheat in PVP

Avoid cheating in PVP, this does not refer exactly to the use of exploits and hacks
The following situations are considered but not limited to:
  • Use mechanics or perform actions that give you advantages over others
  • Generate your life in a way that it is difficult or impossible to kill by any player
  • Use avatars with a hitbox that does not correspond and derived
Do not look for or interpret the information, it is clear what is being said.

Do not try to crash the server

It does not need much explanation about what it is about, it consists of performing actions that make the server's performance lower, in other words, generate lag.

No anti-religious comments

Avoid hatred of religion.

No toxicity

This considered but not limited to toxicity:
  • Insulting someone without reason or "because you don't like that person".
  • Excuses like "It was a joke" will be ignored.
  • Harassing someone who has just entered the server for the first time with insults/swear words.
  • Psychologically making someone feel by comparing him to another person with the reason of making him feel bad
